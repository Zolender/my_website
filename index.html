The Cradle of Humanity

Chad is a landlocked country in central Africa, bordered by Libya to the north, Sudan to the east, the Central African Republic to the south, and Cameroon, Nigeria, and Niger to the west. Covering over 1.28 million square kilometers, it is the fifth-largest country in Africa. This diverse nation boasts a wide range of geographical features, including the Sahara Desert, the Chad Basin, and various savannahs. Each region offers a unique environment, rich in both natural beauty and wildlife, making Chad a fascinating destination for adventurers and nature lovers alike.
